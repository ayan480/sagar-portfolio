Stock Pathshala is a SEBI-compliant stock market education institute founded by Sagar Chowdhury, a NISM Certified Research Analyst. The platform bridges the gap between financial markets and the common retail investor — especially for Bengali-speaking communities across India.

Our philosophy: "An informed investor is a profitable investor." Every session is designed to be practical, honest, and immediately actionable in live markets.
