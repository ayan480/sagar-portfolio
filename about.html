Stock Pathshala — স্টক পাঠশালা

Stock Pathshala is a SEBI-compliant stock market education institute founded by Sagar Chowdhury, a NISM Certified Research Analyst. Our mission is to make quality, practical financial education accessible to every Indian — especially Bengali-speaking retail investors who deserve the same knowledge as institutional traders.

Our Story

Stock Pathshala was born from a simple observation — millions of retail investors in India lose money not because markets are unfair, but because they lack proper education. Sagar Chowdhury set out to change that.

Starting with YouTube videos in Bengali, and growing into a full institute offering live classes, mentoring, and NISM coaching — Stock Pathshala today serves hundreds of students across West Bengal and beyond.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower every retail investor with the knowledge, discipline, and tools needed to navigate Indian financial markets confidently — and profitably.

Who is Sagar Chowdhury?

NISM Certified
Sagar Chowdhury
Research Analyst
West Bengal, India

Sagar Chowdhury is a NISM Certified Research Analyst (Series XV) based in West Bengal, India. With a deep passion for financial markets and a mission to make stock market education accessible to every Indian, he founded Stock Pathshala (স্টক পাঠশালা).

Having navigated Indian equities through multiple bull and bear market cycles, Sagar brings real-world experience to every session. He teaches practical strategies grounded in live market conditions — not just textbook theory.

His YouTube channel @sagarchowdhury-1991 delivers daily market analysis, stock picks, educational series, and F&O strategies primarily in Bengali and Hindi — making quality research accessible to regional investors.

Beyond teaching, Sagar actively conducts equity research, publishes market outlooks, and mentors aspiring analysts — many of whom have gone on to clear their own NISM certifications and build careers in financial markets.
